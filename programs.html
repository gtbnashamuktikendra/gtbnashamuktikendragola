Comprehensive Rehabilitation Programs

Our rehabilitation programs are designed to help individuals overcome addiction through professional treatment, counselling, wellness activities and continuous emotional support.

हमारे पुनर्वास कार्यक्रम नशे से प्रभावित व्यक्तियों को चिकित्सा सहायता, काउंसलिंग, योग, ध्यान तथा सामाजिक सहयोग के माध्यम से स्वस्थ जीवन की ओर वापस लाने के लिए तैयार किए गए हैं।

Our Recovery Process | हमारी सुधार प्रक्रिया

Step 1 : Assessment & Admission

Initial evaluation of physical, mental and emotional condition to prepare an appropriate recovery plan.

Step 2 : Detoxification & Medical Care

Medical supervision and health monitoring to safely manage withdrawal symptoms.

Step 3 : Counselling & Therapy

Individual and group counselling sessions aimed at long-term behavioral transformation.

Step 4 : Rehabilitation & Lifestyle Development

Yoga, meditation, discipline, skill development and healthy routine building.

Step 5 : Family Support & Reintegration

Family counselling and guidance for successful reintegration into society.

Additional Services | अतिरिक्त सेवाएँ

Family Counselling

Helping family members understand addiction and support recovery effectively.

Relapse Prevention

Strategies and guidance to prevent relapse and maintain long-term sobriety.

Motivational Sessions

Regular motivational activities to strengthen confidence and commitment.
