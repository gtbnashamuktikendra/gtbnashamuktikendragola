About Guru Teg Bahadur Educational Society - Nasha Mukti Kendra, Gola

Guru Teg Bahadur Nasha Mukti Kendra is dedicated to helping individuals overcome addiction and rebuild a healthy, productive and meaningful life. We provide professional counselling, medical support, yoga, meditation and rehabilitation services in a safe and supportive environment.

गुरु तेग बहादुर नशा मुक्ति केंद्र का उद्देश्य नशे की लत से प्रभावित व्यक्तियों को स्वस्थ, सुरक्षित और सम्मानजनक जीवन की ओर वापस लाना है। हमारा केंद्र चिकित्सा सहायता, काउंसलिंग, योग, ध्यान तथा पुनर्वास सेवाओं के माध्यम से लोगों को नई दिशा प्रदान करता है।

Our Mission | हमारा उद्देश्य

To help individuals achieve freedom from addiction through professional treatment, emotional support and structured rehabilitation while promoting a healthier and addiction-free society.

Our Vision | हमारी दृष्टि

To create a society where every individual lives with confidence, dignity and good health, free from the harmful effects of addiction and empowered to contribute positively to their families and communities.

Our Goals | हमारे लक्ष्य

To provide comprehensive care and support for individuals struggling with addiction, and to contribute to the creation of a drug-free society through awareness, education and rehabilitation.

Recovery • Rehabilitation • Wellness • New Life
